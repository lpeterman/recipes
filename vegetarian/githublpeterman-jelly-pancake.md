#GitHub Lpeterman's Family Jelly Pancake

## Ingredients
- Butter
- Pancake Mix
- Strawberry Jelly
- Bananas
- Eggs
- Vanilla
- Flour
- Sugar
- Salt

#Directions
1. Beat Eggs with fork until well blended. Add milk and vanilla and beat with a fork.

2. Add flour, sugar and salt. Try and get as smooth as you can.
